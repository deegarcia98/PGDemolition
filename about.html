About Us

Hey there — we’re PG Demolition, and we’re all about helping people clear out the old to make way for the new. The company was founded by and ran by Diego Garcia, who, when he’s not fighting fires as a local firefighter, is leading our crew on demolition projects around town. We take pride in what we do, whether it’s knocking down a backyard shed, clearing out an old building or driveway, or making room for your next backyard patio area. Safety is always our top priority, and we treat every job like it’s happening in our own neighborhood — because most of the time, it is. At the end of the day, we’re just good people doing honest, hard work (with a little bit of fun along the way). If you need something gone, give us a shout — we’ll handle the heavy lifting.
