About Us

At PG Demolition, we’re more than just a construction crew — we’re a family-run business that takes pride in helping other families build, transform, and protect their homes. What started with one man, a strong work ethic, and a commitment to doing things right has grown into a trusted local name in demolition, pool installation, roofing installation and repairs.

We believe in honest work, clear communication, and treating every project like it’s being done for our own family. Whether we're tearing down an old patio, building your dream backyard pool, or replacing a roof to keep your home safe, we show up with integrity, respect, and real craftsmanship.

We’re proud to serve our community and even prouder to be the kind of people you can count on.
